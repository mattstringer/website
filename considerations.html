Considerations

Before we start, let's go through some information to ensure that small-claims court is right for you...

Regulations

  • If you're asking for $35,000 or less, you are eligible to file a claim in Small Claims Court. Anything more and this isn't the court for you.
  • You must file your claim within 2 years of when you first learned about the problem.

Other Solutions

  • Before you decide to sue someone, try other ways of solving the problem such as:
    • Sending a letter asking they pay the money they owe you
    • Getting a mediator to help resolve your issue

Suing isn't always the best option. Here are some reasons you may not want to sue:

  • In most cases, you have to pay court fees.
  • If you lose, the court could order you to pay some or all of the legal costs of the person you tried to sue.
  • It may take a long time, even over a year, and involve a lot of work for you.
  • If you win but the person you sued doesn't pay, it's up to you to collect the money.

If you've reviewed these considerations and think that small claims court right for you, then let's continue!
