Step 2: Serve

It's best if everyone knows

Now that you have filed your claim to the court, it's time to notify the defendant (person being sued).

Keep in mind that once a claim has been submitted and aproved by the court, you have unitl 6 months to serve the defendant.

When serving the defendant (person being sued), it is important to know that you must provide them with the required documentations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The documents that you must present are:

1) A copy of the Stamped Plaintiff Form - so that they have proof that the claim being presented is authentic and real

2) Copies of ALL the other documents you filed with the court - So that they are aware on what grounds that they have been sued

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When you serve a claim, you can serve the claim in person or hire a process server to do it for you.

You may also serve the claim on a defendant using registered mail or a courier.
