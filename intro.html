4 Steps to Justice

Need to go to small-claims court, but unsure of where to start? By following these 4 steps, your small-claims court case will be much stronger.


1. File

You've decided to go to small-claims court. The first step is to file your claim. This includes outlining your case and submitting the proper forms.

2. Serve

When you take someone to court, you have to let them know. This is known as serving documents. When you serve the other party, it has to be done in the proper way with the proper documents.

3. Affidavit

After you serve the documents, you must sign a paper promising that you served. This paper is known as an Affidavit. Preparing an Affidavit is simple; just tell the truth and do it early.

4. Appear

You've prepared your claim by following the previous steps. Now the only thing left to do is show up! Make sure that you show up to your court date on time, and are prepared with your documents.


Get these 4 things done and we're confident you're maximizing your chances of winning. Don't worry, we're here to help. Let's get started!
