Step 4: Appear

This is the final step

Follow this checklist when getting ready for your trial:

  • Review the claim, defence (if any) and any other documents that have been filed.
  • List the points you need to prove and consider how you will prove them.
  • Gather the original documents you need and organize them in logical order.
  • If you have not already done so, serve to all other parties the documents and witness statements that you will use at least 30 days before trial, and include contact information and the office address for each document’s author.
  • Contact any witnesses you need and prepare them.
  • Prepare a list of questions that you would like to ask the opposing parties' witnesses.
  • If you decide to summons any witness(es), serve a copy of the summons and attendance money on your witness(es) at least 10 days before the trial.
  • Get statements from expert witnesses, if you need any. Then, serve the copies to the other parties along with the expert’s contact information, office address, and qualifications.
  • On your trial date, dress appropriately and arrive early.
If you have prepared your case well, you will be much more relaxed in court and you will be able to present your case to its best advantage.
