Starting your claim.

First you will have to submit an 7A Plaintiff's Claim Form.

Some basic information about claims form:

Where applicable, If the defendant is an employee or owner of a company you may want to include both the defendants name and the company name on the form.

Check the company name and address. Sometimes companies don't called by their legal name. Alternatively use the companies corporate number.

For reasons list things such as: what you're asking for and why, the terms of the contract and any terms that weren't followed, when you signed or confirmed the contract, what you have done to try to fix the problem.

You may claim up to $35,000 in damages in small claims court.

You claim may be for money from things such as: returned goods, damage to a product you recieved, costs you paid to repair the product, injury resulting from broken product, Costs of lawyers or paralegals you hired to help.



If there may be more than one plaintiff or defendant you should also complete an Additional Parties Form.
